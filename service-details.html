SIMPLE STEPS FOR OUR LANDSCAPE WORK

01 | Design consultation

We begin with an in-depth consultation to hear your ideas, priorities, and budget. We evaluate the site, discuss style preferences, and identify any functional needs so the concept fits your lifestyle and the space.

02 | Design & planning

Our designers create a tailored plan that includes layout options, plant palettes, materials, and a preliminary budget and timeline. You’ll receive visuals and recommendations so you can see how the final garden will look and function.

03 | Implement construction

After you approve the design, our skilled crew brings the plan to life — grading, hardscaping, planting, irrigation, and installing features. We manage the entire build carefully to keep the project on schedule and on budget.

04 | Garden decorating

With construction complete, we add finishing touches — mulching, decorative accents, final planting, and cleanup — then walk you through maintenance tips and care instructions so your new landscape thrives.
