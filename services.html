Why Choose Rayotec's Low Profile Overlay?

Rayotec's Low Profile Overlay kit includes our thinnest overlay panels which are manufactured from high density XPS 300kPa which is thermally insulated, waterproof and covered with a reinforced cement coating and is designed to be installed onto an existing subfloor to make retrofitting easier.

The system can then be covered by a flexible latex self levelling compound for a smooth finish to lay your final flooring onto. They can operate as a primary heat source depending on heat loss of the room and can work with all types of floor coverings including carpet, vinyl, laminate, engineered wood and tiles.

Perfect for Retrofit Applications

At only 18mm thick this is our lowest profile overlay system which in turn means that your overall build up will be lower. This kit is ideal for someone who is trying to keep within limited boundaries in existing rooms e.g low ceilings or matching height with flooring of other rooms.

About Our Pert-Al-Pert Pipe

Premium 5-layer composite pipe for superior performance

Our Premium Pert-Al-Pert Pipe is a super high quality multi-layered pipe for use with water underfloor heating. This Premium 5 layer Pert-Al-Pert pipe is used in our warm water underfloor heating kits and offers fantastic suitability for underfloor heating by combining the best elements of a plastic and metal pipe into one product.

The aluminium core provides strength and importantly acts as a barrier to stop oxygen getting into the system which can cause premature wear to the manifold. The aluminium layer also makes the pipe form-stable, making it very easy to work with by eliminating spring back for easier installation.

The majority of customers will go for the 16 x 2mm pipe as this is suitable most installations including under screed and should also be used in our 22mm overlay boards (retrofits) and Aluminium spreader plates (suspended floors). The 12 x 1.6mm should be used when looking to achieve the lowest profile retrofits in combination with our 18mm overlay boards.

Superior Construction

  • 5 layer composite pipe using PE-RT inner and outer layers
  • 0.25mm thick edge-welded aluminium core
  • Butted welded manufacturing method
  • Manufactured to meet CE & ISO9001 European & International standards

Performance Benefits

  • Can be formed by hand and maintains its shape
  • Perfect for underfloor heating pipework
  • High temperature resistance
  • Maximum sustained working temp up to 70°C
  • Maximum transient temperature up to 85°C
  • Oxygen barrier prevents premature manifold wear
